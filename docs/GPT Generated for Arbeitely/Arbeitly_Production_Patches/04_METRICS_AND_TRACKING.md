# Arbeitly – Metrics & Tracking

## Purpose
Defines the most important metrics for product, operations, and admin oversight.

---

## 1. Candidate Metrics
- Total self applications
- Total managed applications
- Used quota
- Remaining quota
- Interviews
- Offers
- Rejections
- Conversion rate from applied to interview
- Conversion rate from interview to offer

---

## 2. Employee Metrics
Admin explicitly wants to judge employees by:
1. Total applications completed
2. Interview rate
3. Job pool contributions

Additional metrics:
- Total candidates managed
- Documents generated
- Rejection rate
- Offer rate
- Applications over time
- Average time from To Apply to Applied

---

## 3. Platform Metrics
- Total free candidates
- Total paid candidates
- Free to paid conversion rate
- Total applications sent
- Revenue over time
- Revenue per plan
- Average quota usage by plan
- Job pool volume
- Duplicate rate in job pool

---

## 4. Operational Metrics
- Failed application count
- Failed generation count
- Retry rate
- Stale To Apply count
- Candidate accounts with invalid credentials
- Jobs sitting in pool without action

---

## 5. Suggested KPI Priority

### Super Admin
- Revenue
- Free to paid conversion
- Employee interview rate
- Candidate quota utilization

### Employee
- Interview rate
- Applications completed
- Pool contribution count

### Candidate
- Progress visibility
- Interviews
- Offers
- Remaining managed quota
